About i.c stars

i.c.stars works to activate a technology community of change agents to power social and economic freedom. Working with low-income adults, providing them with employment opportunities and preparing them for community-based advocacy allows us to achieve our social purpose.

Who are we?
An untapped talent pool.

i.c.stars |* Chicago is a technology & leadership training program. It connects underserved and underrepresented people from the Chicago area with the necessary business, leadership and technology training they need to launch themselves into professional careers.

How can you hire from us?
We offer employers options to hire in the following ways:
  • Full-time employee referrals
  • Contingent workforce resources
  • Project-based consultants
